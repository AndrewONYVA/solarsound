HOW IT WORKS — every frame, a ring of sample points is read around the frame's centre, at the iris radius you set. Each point around the ring drives one band; its brightness is quantised to a note in the chosen scale and rendered as a smooth tone. Bright points play loud, dark points stay near‑silent — the same principle 1920s optical film soundtracks used, with a photocell reading light through moving film to make sound. Turn on MIDI Out to send the same notes to a synth, DAW, or hardware instrument of your own instead of (or alongside) the built‑in tones.
